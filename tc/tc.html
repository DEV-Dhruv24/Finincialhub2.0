Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are the income tax slabs in India?

The income tax slabs in India vary based on income and age. For individuals below 60 years, the slabs are as follows:

  • Income up to ₹2.5 lakh: No tax
  • Income from ₹2.5 lakh to ₹5 lakh: 5%
  • Income from ₹5 lakh to ₹7.5 lakh: 10%
  • Income from ₹7.5 lakh to ₹10 lakh: 15%
  • Income from ₹10 lakh to ₹12.5 lakh: 20%
  • Income from ₹12.5 lakh to ₹15 lakh: 25%
  • Income above ₹15 lakh: 30%

How is the tax calculated?

The tax is calculated based on the income slabs mentioned above. The tax amount for each slab is calculated separately and then summed up to get the total tax amount.
